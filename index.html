Nitrogen Plant

Nitrogen gas Produces from atmospheric Air using carbon molecular sieves by PSA Technology. Plant is twin tower Carbon / stainless steel vessel filled with Carbon molecular Sieves. When Air is passes through CMS bed Nitrogen comes out through one tower while other tower is simultaneously regenerata by de-pressurization to Atmosphere. We can get Nitrogen Purily upto 99.9% by This simple PSA. Also we can produce nitrogen upto 99.9999% after adding down stream system called De-oxo/Cu-De-oxo system

Oxygen Plant

Oxygen gas Produces from atmospheric Air using zeolite molecular sieves by PSA Technology. Plant is twin tower Carbon / stainless steel vessel filled with zeolite molecular Sieves. When Air is passes through ZMS bed Oxygen comes out through one tower while other tower is simultaneously regenerata by de-pressurization to Atmosphere. We can get Oxygen Purily upto 95%
